Golden Gate Bridge

The Golden Gate Bridge is a famous suspension bridge in San Francisco, opened in 1937. It's known for its iconic red color and stunning views.

Niagara Falls

Niagara Falls is a famous set of waterfalls on the border of the U.S. and Canada. Known for its stunning beauty, it attracts millions of visitors each year.

Effil Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a famous iron tower in Paris, France, built in 1889. It’s one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world.

Indian Gate

India Gate is a war memorial in New Delhi, India, built in 1931. It honors soldiers who died in World War I and is a prominent national symbol.
